Artist

Through the exploration of 2D and 3D digital design tools, and web development. I create illustrations in Photoshop and Illustrator, 3D modeling in Maya, Blender and Fusion 360, and interactive websites use Html, CSS and JS. I design graphics and sometimes use coding to transfer them in an immerse virtual place. I use anthropomorphic animals, textures, and mimicking things. My curiosity, imagination, insight in life, exploring the dramatic in art and technology, creating hyperreal places transform delightful energy.

I apply the nuance difference elements find in life, and the wonder of the natural environment. I simulate a scene in either fairytales like or abstract form. In my Nature Filter project, I create a one click image filter tool, conveniently retouch the flower photos taken on my journey. I use coding to add effects to the web, the splash in the loading page, the distort effect and different color palette on the photos represents the passage of time.
