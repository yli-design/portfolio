"Nature Filter”, Code Art using P5.js, 2021

Even with the smart photo editing tools, some people wish for a more convenient way to edit the photos. Like one click smart fix all. Therefore, I made this one click filter tool for the beautiful flower photos I took when I went on a trip on Spring break. This interactive website is like a Plog for the trip, a sense of passage of time for nature and me. Explore the image filters both technically and in nature.
